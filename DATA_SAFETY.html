🧘 ZenFocus - Data Safety & Privacy Information

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

App Package: com.codeaxcel.zenfocus

📊 Data Collection

Types of Data Collected

ZenFocus collects the following data to provide and improve its services:

1. App Usage Data
  • Focus session duration and frequency
  • Selected focus modes (Study, Work, Sleep, Custom)
  • App blocking history (which apps were blocked)
  • Feature usage (timer, unlock attempts, PIN usage)
  • Crash reports and error logs
2. Device Information
  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifier (for analytics only)
3. Purchase & Billing Data
  • In-app purchase history
  • Subscription status (Free/Pro)
  • Transaction details (processed by Google Play Billing)
4. Advertising Data
  • Ad impressions and clicks (Google Mobile Ads)
  • Advertising identifier
  • Anonymized user interest data
5. Accessibility Service Data
  • Running app process names (to enforce app blocking)
  • Window state events (for lock screen activation)
  • NO personal information or text content is collected

🎯 How We Use Your Data

  • Functionality: Enable app blocking, timer management, and focus session tracking
  • Analytics: Understand app usage patterns to improve features
  • Personalization: Remember user preferences and settings
  • Advertising: Serve relevant ads to free users (via Google Mobile Ads)
  • Support: Troubleshoot crashes and technical issues
  • Billing: Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Security: Prevent abuse and ensure app stability

🔐 Permissions & Why We Need Them

INTERNET

✓ Required for: Google Mobile Ads, crash reporting, analytics

SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW

BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE

✓ Required for: In-app purchase processing via Google Play Billing

🤝 Data Sharing & Third Parties

ZenFocus does NOT sell or share personal data with third parties. However, the following services may process data:

Third-Party Services:

  • Google Mobile Ads: Serves advertisements and collects anonymous ad data
  • Google Play Billing: Processes in-app purchases (billing only)
  • Google Crash Reporting: Analyzes application crashes
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled): Anonymous usage statistics

These services are bound by their respective privacy policies and comply with GDPR and other regulations.

🛡️ Data Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Local Storage: User preferences and session data stored locally on device
  • No Backend Server: Minimal server-side data storage
  • PIN Protection: Sensitive features protected by user-set PIN
  • Permission Minimization: Only essential permissions requested
  • Regular Updates: Security patches deployed promptly

👤 Your Privacy Rights

Data Access:

Users can view all collected data through the app settings (Settings → Privacy).

Data Deletion:

  • Uninstall the app to delete all local user data
  • Clear app cache in device settings to reset analytics
  • Contact us to delete associated account data (if any)

Opt-Out Options:

  • Ads: Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" in Android Settings
  • Analytics: Disable analytics in app settings (Settings → Data)
  • Crash Reports: Disable auto-crash reporting in settings

GDPR & CCPA Compliance:

ZenFocus respects user privacy rights under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other regulations. Users can request:

  • Data access and portability
  • Right to be forgotten / deletion
  • Restriction of processing

⏰ Data Retention Policy

  • Local App Data: Retained until app is uninstalled
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 24-90 days (varies by provider)
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 30-90 days
  • Ad Data: Subject to Google Mobile Ads retention policy
  • Billing Data: Retained per Google Play Policy and tax requirements

👶 Children's Privacy

ZenFocus is not intended for children under 13 (or applicable age of digital consent in your country).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • If we discover we've collected child data, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents/guardians concerned about data collection can contact us
